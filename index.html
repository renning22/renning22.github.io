Ning Ren

At 10, I was fascinated by how computer games worked. With no internet, I taught myself C programming from books, creating programs with pure curiosity and wonder.

After years in academia and industry, I realized I'd lost that initial spark. My knowledge had grown, but it became constraints that limited my creativity.

The Tao Te Ching says: "為道日損，損之又損，以至於無為，無為而無不為"—in pursuing wisdom, we must unlearn, reduce rather than accumulate, until effortless creation becomes possible.

Today, LLMs and AI agents offer a chance to reimagine everything in CS. This website is my attempt to return to that 10-year-old mindset and recreate that sense of wonder.

I'm founding VibeTrading.dev—where trading meets AI agents. This site will showcase my ideas and experiments exploring how autonomous agents can craft software from scratch.
